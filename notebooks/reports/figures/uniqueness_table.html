Chimera vs. Standard Recommendation Systems

A one-page comparison that frames the project as a production decision system rather than a generic recommender.

Uniqueness summary

How Chimera changes the objective, validation, and operating model.

Dimension Standard recsys Chimera
Objective Maximize proxy relevance or predicted click probability. Maximize incremental retailer margin under utility constraints.
Scoring logic Single ranking score, often opaque to stakeholders. Weighted sum of relevance, uplift, margin, and context.
Business control Recommendation quality is discussed after deployment. Margin, promotion context, and habit strength are explicit levers.
Validation Offline accuracy is the main proof point. Incremental Precision@5, ablation, and simulated A/B lift.
Operations Model retraining is sometimes ad hoc. Weekly retraining, drift thresholds, and rollback gates are defined.
